01 Strap Material
Sublimation gives the sharpest full-colour prints. Satin feels premium & silky. Polyester is the most durable and cost-effective for bulk orders.
02 Strap Width
15mm — slim, lightweight. 20mm — most common, fits most ID holders. 25mm — wide, great for detailed logos and text. 38mm — billboard-width for maximum visibility.

07 Attachment & Accessories
Swivel hook — standard J-hook, most versatile. Bulldog clip — strong spring clip, great for thick ID cards. Retractable reel — extends to swipe card, retracts automatically. Split ring — key-ring style. No connector — plain strap end.
A breakaway clip snaps open under pressure — important for safety in environments with machinery, or as a general best practice. Highly recommended.
Clear PVC card holder fits standard ID / access cards. Choose hard (rigid) or soft (flexible) PVC. Select "None" if you only need the strap.
